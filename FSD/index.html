Alumni Speaks

Mr Ruturaj Radhakrishna Trivedi ,Alumni Mechanical Batch 2013-14

MS (ADVANCED SHIP DESIGN AND HYDRODYNAMICS) MECHANICAL DESIGN ENGINEER- ORTEC GROUP-FRANCE Starting the first year of engineering with computer science is my earliest memory of Orchid College of Engineering. Switching to mechanical engineering in the middle of the third semester was challenging yet fascinating. The professors and staff of the mechanical department were always welcoming. Strict schedules, discipline and the urge to excel were the values of the department and the institute. Though the opportunities for placement were limited back then the department motivated the students to gain technical excellence by preparing for the GATE exam. Innovative efforts like the International Rotary Youth Exchange Program and Study Abroad Cell differentiated the department and the college from the crowd. All this helped me develop as an engineering professional and motivated me to look for opportunities to specialize and work in India and abroad.


Mr Paresh P Gavasane,Alumni Mechanical Batch 2011-12

RTO INSPECTOR: CLASS 1 OFFICEE I am from the first generation of NKOCET. A college where I reinvented myself . It always has been second home for me where study , sports & extracurricular activities goes hand in hand . One thing I would specially like to say staff always used to take extra efforts for backlogged students and I am the beneficiary . Experienced and hard working staff of mechanical branch took lots of efforts to improve the practical and theoretical knowledge of students we got it very useful in further exams.In short NKOCET is the light house for students which emits radiations of knowledge and wisdom . Thank you
